Pepperoni pizza

Description

This classic pepperoni pizza features a crispy, golden crust topped with a rich tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and generous slices of spicy pepperoni.

Baked to perfection, the pizza combines savory, cheesy goodness with a hint of smoky spice from the pepperoni, making it a beloved favorite for any pizza lover. Perfect for a quick dinner, game night, or casual gathering.

Ingredients

  • 1 ball of store-bought pizza dough (about 200-250g) or homemade dough
  • 1/3 cup tomato sauce (pizza sauce or marinara)
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • About 20-25 thin slices of pepperoni
  • 1 tablespoon (for brushing the crust)
  • A pinch of dried oregano or Italian seasoning (optional)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves fopr garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface into a circle about 10-12 inches in diameter.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella evenly over the sauce. Arrange the pepperoni slices on top.
  5. Sprinkle a pinch of dried oregano or Italian seasoning over the pizza for extra flavor.
  6. Transfer the pizza to a baking sheet or pizza stone. Brush the crust lightly with olive oil. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  7. Remove from the oven. Add fresh basil leaves and red pepper flakes if desired. Let it cool for a minute, then slice and enjoy!

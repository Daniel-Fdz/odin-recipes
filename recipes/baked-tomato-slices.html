Baked tomato slices

Description

These baked tomato slices are a simple, healthy, and flavorful side dish or appetizer. Ripe tomatoes are sliced and seasoned with herbs, garlic, olive oil, and a sprinkle of cheese, then baked until tender and slightly caramelized.

Perfect as a light snack, a topping for toast, or a complement to pasta or grilled meats. Quick to prepare and full of Mediterranean flavor, this dish brings out the natural sweetness of tomatoes in every bite.

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 1 medium tomato
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 small garlic clove, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Wash and slice 1 medium tomato into slices about 1/4 inch (0.5 cm) thick. Try to keep the slices even so they bake evenly.
  3. Line a small baking tray with parchment paper or lightly grease it. Place the tomato slices in a single layer, slightly spaced apart.
  4. Drizzle 1 teaspoon of olive oil over the slices. Sprinkle with minced garlic (or garlic powder), dried oregano or Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper to taste.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for about 12–15 minutes, or until the tomatoes are soft, slightly shriveled at the edges, and starting to caramelize.
  6. Remove from the oven and, if desired, sprinkle with fresh basil or parsley before serving. Enjoy warm or at room temperature.

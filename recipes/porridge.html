Porridge with nuts and orange

Description

This warm and comforting porridge combines creamy oats with the satisfying crunch of mixed nuts and the fresh, citrusy brightness of a slice of orange. Perfect for a nutritious breakfast, it’s easy to prepare and packed with fiber, healthy fats, and vitamins.

The natural sweetness of the orange complements the hearty oats and earthy nuts, creating a balanced and delicious start to your day.

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup milk or water (or a plant-based milk)
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2 tablespoons mixed nuts (e.g., almonds, walnuts, pecans), roughly chopped
  • 1 slice of orange
  • 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • A pinch of cinnamon (optional)

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan, combine 1/2 cup rolled oats, 1 cup milk (or water/plant-based milk), and a pinch of salt. Bring to a gentle boil over medium heat.
  2. Reduce heat to low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for about 5–7 minutes until the oats are soft and the mixture thickens to your liking.
  3. Stir in a pinch of cinnamon and 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup if you like a bit of sweetness.
  4. Pour the porridge into a bowl. Sprinkle the chopped mixed nuts on top.
  5. Place a fresh slice of orange on the side or on top as a bright, citrusy garnish.
